#graph-theory 

An ear-decomposition of a graph $G$ is a sequence $G_0 ⊆G_1 ⊆···⊆G_k$ of graphs, such that 
- $G_0$ is a [[cycle]], 
- for each $i= 1,...,k$ the graph $G_i = G_i−1 ∪P_i$, where $P_i$ is a $G_{i−1}$-[[H-path|path]], i.e., $P_i$ is an [[ear]] of $G_i$ , and 
- $G_k = G$ .
